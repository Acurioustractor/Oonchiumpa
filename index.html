Executive Summary

Oonchiumpa has successfully implemented a culturally responsive model that addresses the complex needs of Aboriginal youth in Alice Springs and surrounding communities. This strategic plan outlines our approach to secure a 3-year funding extension from NIAA to solidify programme outcomes and expand services.

Community Safety

Reduction in night-time youth presence in CBD areas and decreased police contact for programme participants

Youth Justice Diversion

Specialised case management for youth at risk of justice system involvement and culturally informed alternatives to detention

Cultural Resilience

Aboriginal-led governance and programme delivery with cultural strengthening initiatives that build youth resilience
