3-Year Implementation Plan

Oonchiumpa's strategy for the next three years focuses on sustainability and expansion to maximize our impact for Aboriginal youth. This timeline outlines our key milestones and activities across each quarter.

Year 1: Foundation Strengthening

Select a quarter to view details

Sustainability Strategy

To ensure long-term impact, Oonchiumpa will:

  • Organisational Capacity

    Strengthen organisational capacity through staff training and systems development

  • Formal Partnerships

    Formalize partnerships with government agencies and community organisations

  • Diverse Funding

    Develop sustainable funding streams beyond grant dependency

  • Cultural Knowledge

    Institutionalize cultural knowledge systems within service delivery frameworks

  • Evidence Base

    Build evidence base through rigorous evaluation

Expansion Strategy

Click on a location to see expansion details

Our expansion will:

  • Increased Capacity

    Increase service capacity to support more youth

  • Geographic Reach

    Broaden geographic reach to include remote communities

  • Service Offerings

    Develop additional service offerings (e.g., vocational training, family healing centres)

  • Safe Spaces

    Create a regional network of culturally safe spaces

  • Training Academy

    Establish a training academy for Indigenous youth workers
