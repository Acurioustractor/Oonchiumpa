## Recommended Measurement Tools

### Cultural Impact Assessment
**Tool:** Adapted "Cultural Connection Scale"
- Connection to country (1-5 scale)
- Language learning/use (yes/no + which languages)
- Elder relationship quality (1-5 scale)
- Traditional knowledge gained (open-ended + categories)
- Cultural identity strength (1-5 scale)

**Frequency:** Baseline (program start) → 3 months → 6 months → 12 months

### Youth Wellbeing Indicators
**Tool:** Culturally adapted wellbeing tool (co-designed with community)
- School attendance (days per week)
- Positive activities participation (count per month)
- Family connection (1-5 scale)
- Community involvement (1-5 scale)
- Future goals clarity (yes/no + description)

**Frequency:** Monthly check-ins (brief), Quarterly assessment (detailed)

### Program Activity Tracker
**Required Fields:**
- Date, time, duration
- Service area
- Activity type
- Location (on-country yes/no, specific site)
- Participants (count, age range, language groups)
- Elders involved (names, roles)
- Traditional knowledge shared (themes, not sacred content)
- Outcomes observed (qualitative notes)
- Photos/videos captured (file names)

**Frequency:** Every activity (real-time logging)

### Partnership Effectiveness
**Metrics:**
- Referrals made (count per partner, per month)
- Successful service access (%)
- Time to service (average days)
- Client satisfaction (1-5 scale)
- Cultural competency of service (1-5 scale, annual assessment)
- Joint initiatives developed (count, descriptions)

**Frequency:** Monthly referral tracking, Quarterly partnership review
